# Venture Thesis — The Delta Playbook

## What it is
A comprehensive PDF guide: "The Delta Playbook: All you need to know about catching Bass on the California Delta." Digital product leveraging Ryan Nichols' expertise in Delta bass fishing. Sold via a simple one-pager landing page.

## Why now
- Ryan has deep expertise and a curated library of YouTube sources (playlists + channels)
- No competing paid PDF product exists in this niche (verified via market research)
- Fishing guides/charters charge $250-500/day — a $27-47 PDF is a no-brainer alternative
- Digital product = zero marginal cost, instant delivery, scalable
- Weekend-launchable: content compilation + design + landing page

## Revenue model
- Price point: $27-47 (impulse buy range for hobbyists)
- Distribution: Direct outreach to fishing communities, social media, potentially paid ads later
- Upside: Bundle deals, future video course, charter referrals

## First 90 days
Ship v1 PDF + landing page within 7 days. Goal: 10 sales in first 30 days ($270-470 revenue). Validate demand before investing in ads or expansion.

## Key unknowns
- How long will content compilation take? (Finch scraping YouTube transcripts)
- What's the optimal price point? (A/B test $27 vs $37 vs $47)
- Best distribution channel? (Facebook fishing groups, Instagram, Reddit r/fishing, local shops?)

## Decision
Pursuing this over other ideas because:
1. Ryan is the actual expert — authentic authority, not me fabricating expertise
2. Content exists (YouTube sources) — Finch can compile, not starting from zero
3. Zero build complexity — PDF + Gumroad/LemonSqueezy landing page
4. First real venture: low risk, fast iteration, proof of concept for the venture engine
